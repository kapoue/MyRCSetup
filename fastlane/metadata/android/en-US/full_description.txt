Modern Android application to manage and save RC car settings during training sessions at the club.

⚠️ NOTE: This application is currently available in French only.

Main features:
• Complete engine settings management (pinion, crown, ratio)
• Chassis configuration (wheelbase, track, ride height)
• Tire parameters (compound, pressure, brand)
• Shock absorber settings (oil, springs, compression/rebound)
• Automatic final ratio calculation
• Complete session history with date/time
• Modern Material Design 3 interface
• Secure local database

Developed by an enthusiast for the French RC community.
Share your settings, improve your performance!
